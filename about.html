Know Us

Who We Are?

Mithra Cookware is a brand known for producing high-quality, smart, and durable kitchenware designed to meet the needs of modern kitchens. Their product line includes pressure cookers, non-stick pans, kadais, saucepans, tawas, and glassware — all crafted with a focus on safety, performance, and style.

Mithra emphasizes user-friendly designs, heat-efficient materials, and a sleek aesthetic, making cooking more efficient and enjoyable for households and professional kitchens alike. The brand blends innovation with tradition, offering reliable cookware that complements contemporary cooking habits.
